Post-Construction Cleaning in the Greater Toronto Area

New builds and renovations leave behind a layer of fine construction dust, debris, and residue that ordinary cleaning simply can't handle. The Professional Cleaners provides thorough post-construction cleaning across the GTA, turning a finished construction site into a clean, move-in-ready space.

Construction dust settles into every corner — on trim, inside fixtures, along window frames, and across floors. We remove that build-up from top to bottom so the final result matches the quality of the work that went into it.

What post-construction cleaning involves

  • Construction dust and debris removal
  • Surfaces, fixtures, and trim
  • Floors and hard-to-reach areas
  • Window sills, ledges, and frames
  • A final detail clean throughout

Serving contractors and builders across the GTA

We help builders, renovators, realtors, and property owners across Richmond Hill, Vaughan, Markham, North York, Toronto, and the wider GTA. Once the work is done, request a free quote and we'll get the space clean and ready.
